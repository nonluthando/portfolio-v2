ScoreRent

Backend · Live

ScoreRent is a live, iteratively developed rental decision-support web app that helps renters evaluate whether a listing is worth applying for before paying non-refundable application fees.

Live demo Repository

Overview

Renters often pay application fees with limited information about whether a listing is realistic for their income and documentation. ScoreRent provides a structured way to evaluate listings and outputs a confidence score (0–100) and verdict.

The score starts at 100 and is adjusted by affordability, document-fit, and demand. It is a confidence score, not acceptance probability.

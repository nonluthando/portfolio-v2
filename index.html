About

Developer focused on building reliable systems that solve real problems.

I am a Computer Science graduate from the University of Cape Town. Most of my formal coursework was assessed in Java, which gave me strong programming and systems fundamentals. I translated this foundation into Python by building real-world backend systems and deploying them into production.

I built and deployed ScoreRent, a rental decision-support web application that helps renters evaluate listings before paying application fees. This involved backend architecture, database design, automated testing, and deployment.

I also implemented a parallel Monte Carlo optimisation algorithm using Java’s Fork/Join framework, strengthening my understanding of concurrency, task scheduling, and performance scalability.

I am currently expanding into C# and .NET to strengthen my enterprise backend development capabilities.
